ABOUT DIAMONDBACKPLAYS

Hello everyone, my name is Chris, but I'm better known as DiamondbackPlays! I have been playing video games ever since I was young; my first handheld device was a Nintendo DS Lite and my first console was a Nintendo Wii (those were the days...). However, after seeing what PC's could handle and accomplish, I found myself gravitating toward PC gaming, and that's where I am today. I was introduced to Minecraft at a friend's house four years ago, and have been hooked ever since. At about the same time, I stumbled across YouTube and discovered all of its amazing content within. Making YouTube videos as a hobby was something I always wanted to do, because to me, those videos leave a mark on the community that will always be there for people to view and enjoy. I sincerely hope that you check out my YouTube channel and like its content!
